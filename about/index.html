Meet the Founders of CrazyMoon Moving Pictures

Visionaries behind our cinematic storytelling

Swapnil Kotriwar

Founder

Swapnil Kotriwar is an acclaimed actor and graduate of the National School of Drama, recognized for standout performances in Paan Singh Tomar, Court Martial, and Netflix’s Betaal. As the founder of CrazyMoon Moving Pictures, he channels his passion for storytelling into producing meaningful cinema rich in authenticity and artistic depth.

Swapnita Nomulwar

Co-Founder

Swapnita Nomulwar is the creative engine of CrazyMoon Moving Pictures. With a Master’s in Commerce, she blends business acumen with a powerful storytelling instinct. Her visual finesse and strategic insight fuel each production with both artistic soul and commercial strength.

Vinay Rangshahi

Creative Head

Vinay Rangshahi, Creative Head at CrazyMoon Moving Pictures, brings every frame to life with his mastery of visual language and emotional nuance. His keen sense for aesthetics and narrative flow transforms ideas into unforgettable cinematic experiences.

Our Creative Team

Marketing Head

Public Relations Head
